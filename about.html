A fresh approach to the lottery industry

  • Company
  • Team

Geonomics was founded in 2008 by brothers James and Henry Oakes with a vision of bringing new and innovative games to the lottery industry.

Our flagship products — GeoLotto (formerly GeoSweep) and Treasure Hunt — are online lottery games that use location technology to engage a new generation of lottery players. We've been running both games in the UK since the end of 2010 under our own gambling license. We also sell and license our games to lottery operators around the world.

Since our inception as Roboreus (we later rebranded to Geonomics in January 2013), we've grown to a team of over 40 world-class game designers, software engineers, technologists and business experts, all led by an experienced and pragmatic management team.

We're headquartered in London and privately owned.

Our Mission

We're here to change the face of the global lottery industry.

As lottery players age, lottery operators are facing the enormous challenge of replacing their current customer base with the next generation of players. Typically younger and more engaged with technology, this new generation of players is a potential goldmine but their disconnection from traditional lottery gaming presents a hurdle for the growth of future revenue.

We believe the lottery industry needs to create new games that appeal to new sensibilities. And we're here to make that happen.

Values

Change is an opportunity

We're not afraid of change, that's why we're here: innovating in the lottery industry takes courage, determination and intelligence. We've seen things that need to be transformed and we're ready to innovate.

There's always room for improvement

We make world-class products and deliver the highest calibre of services but that doesn't mean we're satisfied with our work so far. We're constantly striving to make our products better.

Our people can do great things

We believe our team of engineers, designers and business operatives are exceptionally gifted. We have a judicious hiring process, give our staff ownership of their work, and promote a relaxed but supremely professional work culture.

Patents

With a US Patent (number 8,167,701 B2) and a further 18 applications pending, our core technology and gaming concept is protected from infringement.

Ownership & Investment

Geonomics is a privately owned company. A seed investment of £40k in July 2008 was followed by a 'Series A' funding round of £15m from the Atlantic Lottery Corporation in October 2011. In December 2012, Tipp24 invested £10m in our second round of institutional funding.

Memberships & Compliance

We hold associate memberships for the World Lottery Association, North American Association of State and Provincial Lotteries (NASPL) and European Lotteries (EL). We are regulated by the UK Gambling Commission and hold PCI compliance certification.

Responsible Gaming

We proudly support GambleAware and GamCare, two organisations that encourage responsible gaming and provide information to help players make informed decisions.

Corporate Social Responsibility

In addition to our commitment to creating innovative games, Geonomics proudly supports Anthony Nolan, the UK blood cancer charity.

Anthony Nolan is a pioneering charity that saves the lives of people with blood cancer who need a blood stem cell, or bone marrow transplant.

Geonomics runs regular staff fundraising events throughout the year, raising thousands of pounds in support of Anthony Nolan's important work.

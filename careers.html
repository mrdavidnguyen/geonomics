Join the family

Hailing from all corners of the globe, the Geonomics team is proud to feature an eclectic mix of talented individuals across its creative, engineering and business teams.

When we hire people, we focus on what really matters: skills, attitude and professionalism. We don't value experience alone, and we don't care how you look, dress or what kind of music you listen to.

We just care how you think.

Why make us so great to work for?

We're genuine pioneers in our field

While the U.S. has its high-flying tech startups, the regulatory constraints of the U.S. gambling industry discourages innovation. Here in London, free of those limitations, we're uniquely positioned as one of the world's leading lottery gaming startups. And we're investing all of our resources to take advantage.

We're young, smart and professional

We might be a young team but we're switched-on: we have extraordinarily high standards for our products and our professional conduct. We encourage collaboration, organisation and a firm adherence to the principles of Agile methodology.

We've created a great work culture

By assembling a team of bright, friendly and like-minded people, we've crafted an office culture that merges a great work ethic with a fun but relaxed social atmosphere. We believe in our team and empower them to perform.

Work Benefits

Private health insurance

We offer optional private health insurance with Bupa. You can have single, couple or family cover.

Childcare vouchers

We operate a government childcare voucher scheme through salary sacrifice. You don’t pay tax or national insurance on the vouchers.

Holiday

In addition to 24-days holiday per year, you also get get an extra day off for your birthday. You can also buy or sell up to 5 additional days of holiday a year.

Cycle-to-work scheme

We're part of the Evans cycle-to-work scheme: you can buy a bike through salary sacrifice and we won’t charge you tax or national insurance. At the end of the hire period, the bike is yours to keep.

Gym membership discount

We offer a monthly allowance of £25/month for any gym of your choice. We also offer a corporate discount for the nearby Oasis gym.

Breakfast & refreshments

Our kitchen is stocked with cereal, fresh fruit, toast and crumpets in addition to a wide selection of drinks from teas and coffees to fizzy drinks. We also have a bar complete with a selection of very interesting spirits (watch out for the Fernet Branca!), beer, cider and wine.

Java Developer

← Back to Careers

Date Posted: 24 July 2013

London, UK  |  Engineering

We are looking for an intelligent and effective Java Web Developer for our Instant Win Games (IWG) team. You will have a passion for building leading web applications with the potential to be used by millions of users.

Stuff you’ll do...

  • Working to tight but achievable deadlines in a team with 3-4 other world-class Java Developers to create, improve and support Java code that powers core capabilities of our instant win products Ensuring that code is clear, robust, well documented, unit tested and peer-reviewed Contributing to architectural discussions on performance, scalability, resilience, maintainability, portability and modularity
  • Code reviewing work done by other developers on the team
  • Discussing requirements with product owners and designers to reach rapid consensus on best designs
  • Technical trouble-shooting, documentation and bug-fixing Experience, Skills and Attributes Required: The bit where we talk about your super hero skills...
  • Solid commercial experience using Java (Standard Edition – no need for EJBs) to develop high traffic AJAX consumer-facing transactional web applications – preferably with at least some of this working in an “agile” environment
  • Good understanding of relational databases (ideally PostgreSQL) and related Java frameworks such as Hibernate and Spring
  • Understanding of performance and scalability issues, including multi-threading
  • Working knowledge of GWT or at least one JavaScript library (such as jQuery or Ext-JS)
  • At least a basic knowledge of Linux/Unix systems, including shell scripting
  • Passion for astute pragmatic software engineering that meets the business’s short- and long-term needs
  • Concise clear written communication skills – preferably demonstrated on your CV!
  • You will have an excellent academic record, including:
    • A 2:1 or above (or equivalent) degree in Computer Science, Maths or a science or engineering discipline from one of the world’s leading universities;
    • 3 or more A-levels at grade A (or equivalent qualifications) – ideally including Maths.

You will be awarded a gold star for...

  • PostgreSQL
  • Apache Wicket
  • Google or Bing Maps API
  • Selenium
  • Source code analysis tools such as CheckStyle, FindBugs and PMD

It’s fun to work here:

Our bright modern office in the heart of London is purple-themed (right down to the custom pool-table) and decorated with a large variety of hats (from many different parts of the world). We have fresh fruit delivered to the office twice a week, and the range of hot drinks on offer in our kitchen is exceeded only by the number of obscure spirits in our eclectic after-work bar. If fitness is your thing, we offer a subsidised cycle purchase scheme and discount gym membership – and have secure bike parking and shower facilities on-site. Alternatively (or additionally) there’s the Thursday Thinker quiz and a roving weekly lunch trip to sample the neighbourhood burritos.

If successful in this role you will be provided considerable opportunity to develop your career in the company. how awesome you are...

Salary

£35,000–£55,000 dependent on skills and experience, plus EMI stock options.

Location

Central London (High Holborn)

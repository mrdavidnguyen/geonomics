Executive Team

Our executive team has over 70 years of combined senior management experience at some of the UK's biggest private, corporate and governmental organisations:

James Oakes

Chief Executive Officer & Co-founder

James Oakes is the CEO of Geonomics and co-founder with his brother Henry Oakes. Holding both a B.A. and MSc in Economics from the University of Kent and the London School of Economics respectively, James enjoyed successful spells as an economic forecaster and financial systems developer before making the decision in 2008 to form a technology startup with brother Henry to disrupt the lottery industry. Possessing a bold vision, an affable manner and a shrewd business mind, he guides the company's global strategy and leads the impressively talented Geonomics team with a distinctly collaborative approach.

Henry Oakes

Sales Director & Co-founder

Henry Oakes is Sales Director of Geonomics and co-founder of the company with his brother James Oakes. Possessing a true entrepreneurial spirit, Henry made the brave leap from school straight into creating a technology startup in order to capitalise on the exciting and immediate opportunity to reinvent lottery gaming. As Sales Director, he serves as the frontline of the Geonomics sales strategy, regularly travelling the globe to engage with lotteries, speak at conferences, and to articulate a vision for an industry in need of disruption and innovation.

Matt Young

Chief Technology Officer

Matt Young is the Chief Technology Officer of Geonomics. The company’s very first employee, Matt’s diverse career before joining Geonomics in 2009 included roles as a technology consultant and software architect before becoming Senior Manager of Technology at Sapient, the publicly-listed global marketing and consulting company. He then went on to become Development Director at Betfair, the world’s largest internet betting exchange. Holding a B.A. in Maths from world-renowned Cambridge University, he leads a team of elite developers, engineers and IT experts who are a direct reflection of his high standards for code, technology and analytical insight.

Helen Crosby

Chief Operations Officer

Helen Crosby is Chief Operations Officer at Geonomics. Joining the company in 2011, Helen brings significant senior management experience to the company from her time as Chief Technology Officer and Director at Patientline, the communication and entertainment service for the NHS, and her role as General Manager and Director of Service Solutions at Openreach, the infrastructure division of BT Group. Responsible for a diverse array of operations at Geonomics, from project and product management to legal to finance, Helen has a degree in French and German from the University of Bristol and an MBA (distinction) from Imperial College London.

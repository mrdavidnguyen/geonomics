Treasure Hunt

Click to dig, win instantly

Treasure Hunt is an instant-win game where players click an interactive map to 'dig' for cash prizes and a key to unlock the Grand Treasure.

It's fast, it's fun

Combining the speed of an instant-win game with the engaging interactive experience of Google Maps, Treasure Hunt allows operators to appeal to new players with a concept that's both unique and familiar.

How to play

1. Search the map

  • Players are shown a Google Map divided into squares called Geos
  • Players can choose any Geo they like: their home, a landmark, their football club, their workplace... whatever they fancy!

2. Dig a Geo

  • Players must be registered and deposit funds before they can play
  • Once registered, users can click any Geo on the map to dig it up for prizes: their home, a famous landmark, their workplace... anywhere!

3. Win instantly

  • Players receive instant feedback if their Geo yields a prize
  • Game odds and prize levels can be configured to the operator's requirements

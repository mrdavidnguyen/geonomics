Click to dig, win instantly

Treasure Hunt is an instant-win game where players click an interactive map to 'dig' for cash prizes and a key to unlock the Grand Treasure.

It's fast, it's fun

Combining the speed of an instant-win game with the engaging interactive experience of Google Maps, Treasure Hunt allows operators to appeal to new players with a concept that's both unique and familiar.

How to play

Features

Multi-platform Gaming

Treasure Hunt runs in any standard web browser and is widely accessible on mobile devices including including the iPhone and iPad as well as Android.

Social Media Integration

Players can easily connect with social media tools like Facebook and Twitter to invite friends to play and boast about their wins!

Customisation options

Change the map, games odds, prize levels, digging avatars, sounds and more. For an added fee, we can even create custom storylines for your game with characters, themes and even a plot!

Game board configuration

Lotteries can choose any number of Geos to place on the map and vary their size. Imagine compensating for different population densities to give your players a better chance of winning!
